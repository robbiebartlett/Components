    This is the transcript of the video Maia Rauhina – LSV Graduate from Whanganui

    A Fresh Start

    Name graphic: Maia, 2019 LSV graduate

    V/O transcript

    “I would say to those who are struggling to get a job, to find direction in life, to join LSV

    At the start it was challenging and a struggle, didn’t understand why we did what we did.

    But then as I started to build with everyone; you start to build a family; you start to build a connection.

    One of the best things I got out of LSV was, probably for the long run, a job. Throughout LSV they hold a work expedition which a lot of employers come around from NZ.

    Having a job will mean a lot for me. It’ll help me grow independently. I’ll be earning my own money which will feel more rewarding and deserving.

    And you grow so much. You don’t see it but the people around you see it and then your families will see it when you get back.

    And at the end you feel so proud to be who you are.”
